Welcome to Capsule Clash!

You might be wondering, what is Capsule Clash?

Capsule Clash is a multiplayer FPS with shotguns, ARs, building, sprinting, jumping, and dashing.

It takes seconds to load into a room and start playing, and you and up to 10 of your friends can play in a single room. So what are you waiting for?
